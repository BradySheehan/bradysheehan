About Me

Currently I am studying mathematics and computer science at Duquesne University in Pittsburgh, PA. I love Pittsburgh and have had an awesome experience studying at Duquesne the last three years.

My favorite hobby is playing billiards. I've played at several pool halls across the state and some of my favorites are listed below (in no particular order)!

- Mully's Billiards
- Drexaline Billiards
- Buffalo Billiards
- Ball Busters Billiards
- Markely Billiards
- Club Med Billiards

You might see me there sometime! I've been playing with the same cue since I was 13 years old. I have a J. Pechauer Cue (more specifically the 2005 Professional Series PS09 ). I love this cue and never plan to replace it. In my senior year of high school I bought an original OB-2 low-deflection shaft to use with the butt of my Pechauer and really enjoy playing with it.

I also have a 2005 Polaris Predator 500 ATV that I love to ride with friends and family. One of our favorite places to ride during the summer is at the Ramey Poker Run.

I love to read and occasionally answer questions on Quora and I often peruse stackexchange.
